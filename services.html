Specialist services


Property Management

Arkle Property Services specialise in property management covering a range of properties, including high end and exclusive properties. There are many different levels of management that we are able to offer including our in-house services.

Property Refresh

We have extensive experience at property refresh with a dedicated professional team who have completed a refresh programme of 900 homes on time and to budget. Property refresh is recommend between tenancies or throughout an extended tenancy to ensure that the property continues to be of a high specification and continues to achieve the market value.

Smart home automation and control systems

We specialise in the design, installation, supply, management & support of luxury, bespoke, custom, high end, executive home, retail, office & boardroom automation installations such as Creston, Reka, Lutron and C4 systems.

Commercial heating

Arkle Property Services offers a full range of services including planned and preventative maintenance, installation and servicing to all makes of boilers and plant whether gas, oil or air handling systems.
We can service any commercial heating appliance and our qualified engineers also have a wide knowledge of the leading brands of boilers.

AC systems

Heating and Air Conditioning equipment, no matter what kind you have, should be inspected, cleaned, and serviced six monthly or annually depending on what type of system you have. The best scenario is to have the heating system checked in preparation for Spring. If living in the city the filters will get blocked and could damage the system.
